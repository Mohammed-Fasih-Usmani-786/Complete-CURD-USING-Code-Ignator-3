Disinfestation Service: Fight against Corona (Covid-19)

Microbial contamination has become global challenge now, UssPestCon is now come with Disinfection service to care your health and hygiene.

Indoor surfaces and air may look and smell clean. But time and again it may be contaminated with harmful microorganisms that contain airborne and viral diseases, such as Corona Viruses, several Flu and several bacterial and fungal diseases which may become fatal or infected person can have life threatening complications, if not cured on time.

How do UssPestCon prevent viral and airborne disease from spreading?

UssPestCon has designed an effective treatment which disinfect the premises and material and sterilize the surfaces which gives you peace of mind. We use a unique formulation with a powerful composition that surpasses all the other disinfectants. In terms of efficacy, it has been proven highly effective against 100 strains of virus in over 24 viral families, 400 strains of bacteria and over 100 strains of fungi, including airborne and viral diseases.

Procedure :

  • Our trained technicians will be fully equipped with PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) for this treatment.
  • An effective disinfectant solution is prepared on the premises and this chemical of trusted Brands like Amway, Diversy, Dupoint etc.
  • Aerial disinfection service will be carried out using Mist Blower machine. Customers have to ensure the covering of all sensitive equipment and food items with plastic sheets. Treatment area to be enclosed and exposed with disinfectant solution for a minimum of 2 hours. Then the treated area should be ventilated for 15 min at least.
